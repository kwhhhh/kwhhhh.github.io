bank branch expansion

Tue Jul 26 2022

Recently I started a small project about the bank branch geographic expansion and the bank’s performance.

Using $285$ banks in China (mostly rural commercial banks or city banks), I find that when a bank has more branches outside the head city of the bank, there is a higher risk of the bank (measured by risk-weighted asset divided by total asset), and higehr bad loan percentage, higher deposit divided by loan, and higher net asset.

The results are robust when the province#year effect and type of the bank#year effect are controlled. (Where province is the province of the bank headquarter is located)
